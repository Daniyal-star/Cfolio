About Me

IIn an age where attention spans are fleeting and digital experiences matter more than ever, I’ve found my passion at the crossroads of AI automation, UI/UX design, and data science. My journey started with data — building predictive models using NumPy and Pandas — but evolved into crafting intuitive interfaces that make those models accessible and impactful. One of my proudest projects is an AI-driven YouTube snippet extractor that intelligently pulls the best parts from long videos based on user input. It’s a tool that blends smart algorithms with clean design — a reflection of how I work. When I’m not behind a screen, I’m either on the tennis court sharpening my reflexes or cycling through scenic routes, drawing inspiration from the world around me.
